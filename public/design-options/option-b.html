FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our virtual office services

What is a virtual office address?
A virtual office provides you with a prestigious business address without the cost of renting physical office space. You can use it for GST registration, company incorporation, business correspondence, and establishing a professional presence in any city.
Can I use a virtual office for GST registration?
Yes! Our virtual office addresses are fully compliant for GST registration. We provide all required documents including NOC, rent agreement, utility bills, and address proof. We have a 97% approval rate for GST registrations.
How long does the setup process take?
Our setup process typically takes 2-3 business days from the time you submit your documents. For urgent requirements, we also offer express processing that can be completed within 24 hours.
What documents do I need to provide?
You'll need basic KYC documents - PAN card, Aadhaar card, passport-size photograph, and a cancelled cheque or bank statement. For company registration, additional documents like MOA/AOA may be required.
Is there a lock-in period?
No, we do not have any lock-in period. Our plans are flexible and you can choose monthly, quarterly, or annual billing. Annual plans come with additional discounts.
Do you handle mail and packages?
Yes, we receive all mail and packages on your behalf. You'll get instant digital notifications and can choose to have them forwarded to your preferred address or pick them up from our center.
